ezyrb.mapper.Mapper.get_cell_centroid¶

static Mapper.get_cell_centroid()¶

Compute the centroid of the cell defined by vertices; the centroid is computed as the vertices coordinates average.

Parameters:vertices (numpy.ndarray) – a n_vertices-by-3 matrix where vertices coordinates are store by row.
Returns:cell centroid coordinates
Return type:numpy.ndarray
